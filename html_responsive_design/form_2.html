Instructions for Application - Academic Year 2014

  1. The deadline for 2014-2015 scholarship application: March 30, 2014. Applications recevied after March 30 will be considered on a funds available basis.
  2. Only completed applications will be considered. Do not Leave Items Blank.
  3. Attach a current copy of your high school and/or college transcripts.
  4. Two signed letters of recommendation (from non-relatives)must be attached to this application.

Consideration for any scholarship award based on a point system including GPA, participation in extra-curricular activities, school organizations and community service. This will be a four semester scholarship, and you must maintain criteria set in the guidelines.
